Let's try to systematically analyze the game.

We have two players. The game is determined by the sequences of x_n. The constraints are cumulative. The players can choose any nonnegative numbers. The game ends if a player cannot move. This is a kind of "game of bounds" where each player can "push" the sum or sum of squares.

Observation: The game is similar to a "game of inequalities" where each player controls a "resource" and tries to exhaust the opponent's budget. The sum condition S_n ≤ λ n for odd n restricts Alice's total sum, while the square sum condition Q_n ≤ n for even n restricts Bazza's total sum of squares.
