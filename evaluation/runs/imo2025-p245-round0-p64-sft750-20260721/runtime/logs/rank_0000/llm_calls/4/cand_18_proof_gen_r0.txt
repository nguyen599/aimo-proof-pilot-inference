Let \(f(N)\) denote the sum of the three largest proper divisors of a positive integer \(N\).  For \(N\) with at least three proper divisors we list its positive divisors in increasing order:
\[
1 = d_0 < d_1 < d_2 < \cdots < d_{\tau(N)-1} = N .
\]
The three largest proper divisors are \(N/d_1,\ N/d_2,\ N/d_3\); therefore
\[
f(N) = \frac{N}{d_1} + \frac{N}{d_2} + \frac{N}{d_3}. \tag{1}
\]
