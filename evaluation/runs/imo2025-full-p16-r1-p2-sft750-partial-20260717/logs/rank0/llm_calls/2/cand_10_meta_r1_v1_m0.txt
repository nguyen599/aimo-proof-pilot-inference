## Solution

E = \Bigl(1-\frac{t_E(1+pq)}{2},\frac{t_E(q-p)}{2}\Bigr),\quad

M=(0,\cot2u)=\Bigl(0,\frac{1-p^2}{2p}\Bigr),\qquad
N=(0,-\cot2v)=\Bigl(0,\frac{q^2-1}{2q}\Bigr),
\]

\[
H = \Bigl(\frac{1-pq}{2pq},\frac{q-p}{2}\Bigr).
\]

\[
(q-p)x+(1+pq)y = \frac{(q-p)(1+p^2q^2)}{2pq}. \tag{1}
\]

d = \frac{\bigl|-\frac{(q-p)(1+p^2)(1+q^2)}{4pq} - \frac{(q-p)(1+p^2q^2)}{2pq}\bigr|}

R_c = \frac{(q-p)\sqrt{(1+p^2)(1+q^2)}}{4pq},
\]
